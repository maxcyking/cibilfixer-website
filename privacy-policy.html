Privacy Policy for Cibil Fixer Admin Panel

Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Last Updated: January 15, 2025
Important: This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the Cibil Fixer Admin Panel Android application. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our admin panel app, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • CIBIL Report Data: Credit scores, financial information, loan details
  • Authentication Credentials: Login details, security tokens
  • Administrative Data: User management activities, system configurations
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app version for functionality

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage Analytics: Features used, session duration, user interactions
  • Technical Data: Crash reports, performance metrics, error logs
  • Security Information: Device identifiers, IP addresses for fraud prevention
  • Location Data: Approximate location for service optimization (with permission)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide CIBIL report management and admin panel services
  • Maintain user accounts and secure authentication
  • Process and manage credit-related applications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure data security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Send important notifications and service updates

Data Storage and Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Secure Storage: All data is securely stored using Firebase Cloud services with enterprise-grade security
  • Encryption: Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access Control: Restricted access to user data by authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Automated daily backups with 99.9% uptime guarantee
  • Compliance: Adherence to Indian IT Act 2000 and RBI guidelines

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our services (credit bureaus, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court orders, or government regulations
  • Fraud Prevention: To protect against fraudulent activities and security threats
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
  • User Consent: When you explicitly authorize information sharing

Third-Party Integrations

Our app integrates with:

  • Firebase: For app infrastructure and analytics
  • Credit Bureaus: CIBIL, Experian, Equifax for credit report services
  • Payment Gateways: Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • SMS Services: For OTP verification and notifications

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request complete removal of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data automatically deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained longer as required by Indian law
  • User Deletion Requests: Complete data removal within 30 days upon request

Children's Privacy

Cibil Fixer Admin Panel is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than India. We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and regulatory compliance.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make changes:

  • We will notify you through the app or email
  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Material changes will require your explicit consent
